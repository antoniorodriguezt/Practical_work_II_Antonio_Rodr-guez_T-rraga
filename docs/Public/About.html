About Me

Nowadays, employability is increasing in areas related to computer science and its derivatives due to the demand for specialists and because of the digitalization of enterprises and businesses. That’s what inspired me to choose these studies. Also, this is very related to my goals: being a successful entrepreneur, launching a digital-services enterprise. That’s why I chose Business with Informatics — I think it’s a productive way to acquire knowledge in areas related to my goals.

In addition, I’d like to say that both sides of my degree have been part of my passions since I was a child, especially the field of computer engineering. From an early age, my father encouraged me to interact with computers, understand how they work, explore basic programming, and other related areas. That’s why I am deeply passionate about the field of computer science.
